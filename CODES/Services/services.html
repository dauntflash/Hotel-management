Loans

We make your dream business come true by providing you with the necessary finances you may require to start your income stream venture.


Indivudual Loans

Members with well established businesses do not neeed group to obtain a loan. The loans are from 200,000 - 5,000,000 depending on the level that the busines is.

Group Loans

We enable groups to expand their current income generating ventures by making available for them finances based on tiers.

  • One-Tier -300,000-1,000,000 with 1.25% interest rates payable within 9months
  • Two-Tier- 500,000-5,000,000 with 1.25% interest rates payable within 12months
  • Three-Tier- 1,000,000-10,000,000 with 1.25% interest rates payable within 18months

Eligibility?

  1. Group MUST have maintained operation with the SACCO in the last 6months
  2. Loans above 500,000 to be secured with a collateral
  3. Have minimum saving of 20% of the total amount being borrowed
